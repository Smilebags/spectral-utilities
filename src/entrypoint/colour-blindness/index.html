The prefixes “protan”, “deutan”, and “tritan” refer to the L-cones, M-cones, and S-cones, respectively.

M sensitivity contribution within L cells L sensitivity contribution within M cells S sensitivity scaling

Protanomaly

L cone response becomes more like M cone. Reds and greens get harder to distinguish.

Protanopia

L cone response is identical M cone. Reds and greens are indistinguishable. Red objects appear darker than they would in Deuteranopia.

Deuteranomaly

M cone response becomes more like L cone. Reds and greens get harder to distinguish.

Deuteranopia

M cone response is identical L cone. Reds and greens are indistinguishable. Red objects appear lighter than they would in Protanopia.

Tritanopia

S cone response is entirely missing. Blue objects appear dark, differentiation between colours containing and not containing blue is lost.
